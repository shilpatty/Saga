Specialist motorhome cover

Our policies are designed by motorhome enthusiasts, for motorhome enthusiasts. Whether you're a weekend explorer or a full-time traveller, we've got you covered.

What does motorhome insurance cover?

Our comprehensive policy covers fire, theft, accidental damage, third-party liability, and personal belongings. European cover is included as standard.

Do I need specialist motorhome insurance?

Yes — standard car insurance won't cover your motorhome properly. Specialist insurance accounts for the vehicle's size, value, and live-in equipment.

Can I drive my motorhome in Europe?

Yes, European cover is included as standard in all our motorhome policies, covering you in EU countries and select non-EU destinations.

How is my motorhome valued?

We use agreed value cover, meaning we agree the value of your motorhome upfront so there's no dispute at claim time.
